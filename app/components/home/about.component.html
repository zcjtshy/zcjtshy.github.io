Welcome to the Soul Relics Museum.
Soul Relics is an outline platform for object-based storytelling and mental health.
Here you will find a gallery of objects and personal stories that relate a difficult time in our contributors’ lives when they struggled with mental ill health or traversed the journey to recovery.
A Soul Relic can be anything of personal significance, and we invite you in to explore the archive and contribute your objects.
Ultimately, Soul Relics is a place for collective storytelling and story-receiving – a repository of memories, feelings and experiences. We hope that by engaging with this archive, you will be able to deepen your relationship with yourself and others.
We would like to invite you to share your story on our website, or if you do not wish to share, have a look around and read the stories of others.
It is a gallery of photos of objects and personal stories that are related to a difficult time of your lives (A period of mental ill health); and/or something that helped you work towards recovery. That object can be anything of personal significance, or/and represent institutional barrier to mental health care, etc.
